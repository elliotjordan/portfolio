These are some of the websites I worked on at Indiana University.

At IU, I worked in 4 different roles (1- Instructional Developer - CEEP, 2- Webmaster - Purchasing, 3- Webmaster - Financial Training, 4- Systems/Web Developer - Budgets).

Since 2011, I began building WordPress-based websites for research teams within the Center for Evalation and Education Policy. These sites included research work done for The Equity Project, Atlantic Philanthropies, and PBIS (Positive Behavioral Instruction Support). I collaborated with many internal and external stakeholders to develop new website features, create new pages and sections, update content, fix bugs and typos, and ensure accessibility.
